Thailand: ASEAN's Detroit

Thailand produces 1.9 million vehicles annually, making it the largest automotive manufacturing hub in Southeast Asia. Behind every vehicle are 2,500+ tier 1-3 suppliers facing relentless demands: perfect quality, complete traceability, and JIT delivery windows measured in hours, not days. DigiWin has spent 20+ years helping these suppliers meet—and exceed—OEM expectations.

The Automotive Stakes Are High

Trace or Lose

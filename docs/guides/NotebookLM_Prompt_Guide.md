## 1. Pain Naming Questions (Track A - Factories)

### Manufacturing Chaos
```
What specific operational problems do Thai manufacturing SMEs face
that DigiWin solves?

Look for references to:
- Production scheduling chaos
- Inventory inaccuracies ("ghost inventory")
- Excel spreadsheet dependency
- Missed shipments or delivery failures
- Manual data entry burden
- Lack of real-time visibility

Extract exact quotes or statistics I can use in hero sections.
```

### Failed ERP History
```
What evidence exists about ERP implementation failures in manufacturing?

Find:
- Statistics on ERP project failure rates
- Reasons why generic ERP fails in factories
- Quotes about past bad experiences with software vendors
- The difference between manufacturing-specific vs. general-purpose ERP

I need this to address the fear: "We tried ERP before and it failed."
```

### Cost of Inaction
```
What is the cost of NOT having proper ERP/MES for a Thai factory?

Find statistics or evidence about:
- Productivity losses from manual processes
- Inventory carrying costs from inaccuracy
- Revenue lost from missed shipments
- Labor costs from redundant data entry
- Compliance risks from poor traceability

I need quantified pain to justify the investment.
```

## 2. Proof & Outcomes Questions (Track A - Factories)

### Measurable Results

## NotebookLM Power Tips

### 1. Pin Your Best Extractions
When NotebookLM gives you a great answer, click "Save to note" to pin it. You'll build a curated library of your best content.

### 2. Generate Audio Overview
Use the audio overview feature to create a "podcast" about your sources. Listen while commuting to internalize the content.

### 3. Ask Follow-Up Questions
NotebookLM remembers context. After a broad question, ask:
- "Go deeper on point 3"
- "Find more examples of that"
- "How would I phrase that for a Thai factory owner?"

### 4. Request Specific Formats
Ask for outputs in the format you need:
- "Format as a bulleted list"
- "Create a comparison table"
- "Write as a testimonial quote"
- "Structure as hero → body → CTA"

### 5. Cross-Reference Sources
Ask NotebookLM to connect sources:
- "What in the case studies supports the claims in the PRD?"
- "Where do the company facts contradict or support the playbook?"

### 6. Challenge Your Content
Use NotebookLM to stress-test:
- "What objections would a skeptic raise to these claims?"
- "Which of these statistics is most/least credible?"
- "What's the weakest argument in this document?"

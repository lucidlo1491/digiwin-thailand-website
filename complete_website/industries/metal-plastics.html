Where Margins Are Made

In metal and plastics processing, material costs are 50-70% of your total cost. Every defective part is money in the scrap bin. Every second of cycle time drift is capacity you'll never recover. Small improvements in yield and efficiency translate directly to the bottom line—but you can't improve what you can't measure.

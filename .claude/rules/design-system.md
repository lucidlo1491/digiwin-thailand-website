# Design System — Auto-loaded every session

## Peter's Design Priorities
1. **Space works hard** — no wasted areas, fill meaningfully or tighten
2. **Visual unity** — connected elements, not scattered
3. **Icons self-explain** — visuals communicate without labels
4. **Thai audience context** — spell out abbreviations, plain language, relatable analogies
5. **Detail without walls** — bullet points, visual hierarchy, scannable
6. **Snappy animations** — 0.4s ease, not 0.6s (users shouldn't wait)
7. **Premium industrial** — Navy #000864, Smart Blue #00AFF0, particle waves, Super D device
8. **Welcoming, not salesy** — "Let's Talk" not "Book a Demo", no product demos offered
9. **Real links only** — check actual files exist before linking

## Typography
Headings: Noto Sans (500-700) | Body: Noto Sans (400) | Labels: JetBrains Mono (uppercase, 0.1em spacing) | Chinese: Noto Sans SC

## Animation Timing
Scroll fade-in: 0.4s ease, stagger 0.07s | Hover: 0.3s-0.4s ease | Wave drift: 30s loop | Super D draw: 1.2s stroke-dasharray

Manufacturing Challenges, Solved

Generic ERP wasn't built for factories. These problems disappear when your system understands manufacturing.

The Problem

"We don't know our true costs until month-end"

Standard cost variance buried in accounting entries. Material waste discovered weeks after production. No visibility into real-time margin.

DigiWin Solution

Real-Time Cost Visibility

Actual cost calculated at each production stage. Margin visibility before you ship, not after you invoice.

The Problem

"MRP gives us garbage because our BOMs are outdated"

Engineering changes happen on the floor, not in the system. BOM versions scattered across spreadsheets.

DigiWin Solution

Engineering Change Control

ECN workflow ensures changes are captured before production. MRP always runs against current, approved BOMs.

The Problem

"Inventory count never matches the system"

Ghost inventory haunts every audit. Production consumes materials that don't exist. Purchasing reorders what you already have.

DigiWin Solution

Transaction-Level Accuracy

Every material movement captured at source. Barcode scanning enforced at key points. Cycle counting built in.

The Problem

"We can't trace quality issues back to their source"

Customer complaints trigger panic investigations. Lot numbers exist somewhere, maybe. Root cause analysis takes weeks.

DigiWin Solution

Complete Traceability

Lot tracking from receiving to shipping. One click shows full genealogy. Quality data linked to batches, machines, operators.

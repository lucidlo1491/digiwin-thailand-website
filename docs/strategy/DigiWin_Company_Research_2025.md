*Compiled: February 2025 | For DigiWin Thailand Internal Use*

## Executive Summary

DigiWin (鼎捷数智) is a publicly-traded enterprise software company specializing in manufacturing ERP, MES, PLM, and intelligent manufacturing solutions. Founded in Taiwan in 1982, the company has grown to become **the #1 domestic ERP vendor in China's manufacturing sector** with operations spanning 15+ countries across Asia-Pacific.

## Complete Historical Timeline (1982-2025)

### The Founding Era (1982-1990)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **1982** | **Company Founded** - Dingxin Computer Consulting Co., Ltd. (鼎新电脑顾问股份有限公司) established in Taipei, Taiwan on March 2nd. Primary focus: software development and consulting services for manufacturing enterprises. |
| **1982-1988** | Adopted open-platform development standards and identified manufacturing as core market focus. Built foundational expertise in production management systems. |
| **1989** | **First Major Product Launch** - Released LEADER, an MRPII (Manufacturing Resource Planning II) software package built on DOS platform. This marked DigiWin's transition from consulting to product-based software company. |
| **1990** | Achieved dominant position in Taiwan's manufacturing software market. Over 70% market share in Taiwan's ERP sector by end of decade. |

### Taiwan Market Dominance (1991-2000)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **1992** | **TIPTOP ERP Launch** - Released TIPTOP, one of the first Browser/Server (B/S) architecture ERP systems in Asia. Revolutionary for its time, enabling web-based enterprise management. |
| **1995** | Expanded product portfolio to include financial management, HR, and supply chain modules. |
| **1997** | Taiwan's enterprise informatization entered rapid growth phase ("龙卷风暴期"). DigiWin established undisputed market leadership in Taiwan's manufacturing ERP sector. |
| **1999** | Began serving Taiwan manufacturers expanding to mainland China, providing cross-strait ERP support. |
| **2000** | Prepared strategic entry into mainland China market through government-coordinated partnership discussions. |

### China Expansion Era (2001-2007)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **2001 Feb** | Established Dinghua Information Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. (鼎华信息技术（上海）有限公司) as mainland operations base. |
| **2001 Apr** | **Taiwan Stock Exchange Listing** - Listed on Taiwan Stock Exchange (stock code: 2447), becoming Taiwan's first ERP-focused listed company. |
| **2001** | **Digital China Joint Venture** - Under coordination of Taiwan Affairs Office and Ministry of Information Industry, formed joint venture with Digital China (神州数码). Created Digital China Management Systems (DCMS) with 60% DigiWin / 40% Digital China ownership. |
| **2002** | **Rapid China Growth** - Within one year of establishment, DCMS ranked in China's Top 3 ERP market. Won "2002 China ERP Software Market Fastest Growing Enterprise" award (赛迪顾问). |
| **2003-2005** | Continuous expansion across mainland China. Established branch offices in major manufacturing hubs: Shanghai, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Suzhou, Dongguan. |
| **2006** | Achieved #1 market share among domestic ERP vendors in China's manufacturing sector (13.6% share). Abandoned Hong Kong IPO plans to focus on mainland market development. |
| **2007** | Maintained market leadership with 14.1% market share. Deepened industry-specific solutions for electronics, machinery, and automotive sectors. |

### Strategic Restructuring (2008-2013)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **2008 Jan** | **Taiwan Voluntary Delisting** - Became the first operationally-healthy Taiwan listed company to voluntarily delist. Decision made to enable deeper China market integration without regulatory restrictions on cross-strait investments. |
| **2008 Sep** | **Group Restructuring** - DCMS and Dingxin Computer merged to form Digiwin System Group Holdings (鼎捷系统集团控股公司). Unified brand identity across Taiwan and China. |
| **2008** | Market share increased to 14.3%. Continued #1 position among domestic manufacturing ERP vendors. |
| **2009 Nov** | **Rebranding** - Digital China Management Systems officially renamed to Digiwin Software Co., Ltd. (鼎捷软件有限公司). Consolidated brand for both markets. |
| **2010** | Launched cloud-based service initiatives. Expanded into Southeast Asian markets through partnerships. |
| **2011 May** | Corporate restructuring to Digiwin Software Co., Ltd. (鼎捷软件股份有限公司) in preparation for China IPO. |
| **2012** | Achieved 30,000+ cumulative customer milestone. Headquarters established in Shanghai Zhangjiang High-Tech Park. |
| **2013** | Completed IPO preparation. Submitted listing application to Shenzhen Stock Exchange ChiNext board. |

### Public Company Era (2014-2019)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **2014 Jan 27** | **Shenzhen Stock Exchange IPO** - Listed on ChiNext board (stock code: 300378). First Taiwan-origin enterprise management software company to list on mainland China exchanges. |
| **2014 Oct** | **MES Expansion** - Strategic investment in AMMA Technology (艾玛科技), a Manufacturing Execution System (MES) specialist. Expanded smart factory capabilities. |
| **2014** | Launched E10, new generation mid-to-large enterprise ERP product. Established Malaysia joint venture for ASEAN market expansion. |
| **2015** | **"Smart+" Strategy Launch** - Announced "智+" strategy with "One Line, Three Rings, Interconnected" (一线三环互联) roadmap for Industry 4.0 transformation. Laid foundation for intelligent manufacturing focus. |
| **2016** | Expanded AIoT (AI + IoT) product development. Deepened integration between ERP and shop floor systems. |
| **2017 Nov** | **Thailand Market Entry** - Partnered with Synmedia Information (新美信息) to host "Smart Manufacturing Implementation Seminar" in Bangkok. Established Thailand as new ASEAN market base following Vietnam and Malaysia. |
| **2018** | 36th anniversary. Continued international expansion across ASEAN region. |
| **2019** | **Next-Gen Product R&D** - Initiated development of new-generation digital intelligence products. Began AI integration research. |

### Digital Intelligence Transformation (2020-2025)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **2020 Jul** | **Foxconn Strategic Investment** - Foxconn Industrial Internet (工业富联, stock: 601138) acquired 15.19% stake for CNY 560 million (39.97 million shares at CNY 14/share). Became largest shareholder with 22.85% combined voting rights through concerted action agreement. |
| **2020** | Deepened industrial internet and intelligent manufacturing capabilities through Foxconn partnership. |
| **2021** | Accelerated AI+Manufacturing integration. Expanded smart factory solution portfolio. |
| **2022 Aug 23** | **Athena Platform Launch** - Released "Digiwin Athena" (鼎捷雅典娜), next-generation Industrial Internet PaaS platform. Named after goddess of wisdom. Encapsulates 40 years of manufacturing industry knowledge into intelligent system. |
| **2022** | **40th Anniversary** - Commemorated four decades of continuous manufacturing software innovation. Won German Red Dot Design Award for Athena platform. |
| **2023 Jun** | **GPT Integration** - Taiwan subsidiary partnered with Microsoft to develop first GPT-powered manufacturing application in Taiwan using Azure OpenAI. |
| **2023** | Became Baidu Wenxin Yiyan (文心一言) first-batch ecosystem partner. Integrated AI capabilities across product lines. |
| **2024 Sep** | **Corporate Rebrand** - Changed official name from "Digiwin Software" to "Digiwin Co., Ltd." (鼎捷数智股份有限公司). Signaled evolution from software company to "Digital Intelligence" solutions provider. |
| **2024** | Integrated Doubao (豆包) large language model for enhanced multi-modal AI capabilities. Revenue exceeded CNY 2.37 billion. |
| **2025 Feb** | **DeepSeek Integration** - Fully integrated DeepSeek large language model into IndepthAI agent platform and all intelligent applications. Enables AI-powered copilots across procurement, finance, design, and equipment management. |
| **2025** | **43 Years of Continuous Operation** - Maintained #1 domestic market share in China's manufacturing ERP, MES, and PLM segments. |
